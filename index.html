Welcome to Skills Builder Institute

Skills Builder Institute is dedicated to practical, hands-on training that prepares learners for real jobs. We provide industry-focused courses, personalized career guidance, and placement support for students and professionals. Our trainers are experienced industry professionals who bring practical skills, mentorship, and real project experience. Students receive certification, resume help, mock interviews, and job-placement assistance to increase hiring chances. We cover technical skills like web development, programming and engineering, as well as trades and soft skills. Our classroom and on-field training ensures learners are job-ready with measurable skills employers want. Over 10,000+ learners trained and 5,000+ placed across local and international employers through our network. Our vision is to expand training across Siwan and Bihar, empowering youth with meaningful careers and stability. Join our programs to build confidence, get certified, and start a reliable career path with active support. Contact us to find the course that fits your goals and start upskilling today.
