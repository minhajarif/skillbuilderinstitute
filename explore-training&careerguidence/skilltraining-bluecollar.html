Blue Collar Skill Training

Our Blue Collar Skill Training programs are designed for learners who want practical, hands-on experience that directly prepares them for jobs in India and abroad. All training is delivered by experienced industry professionals who focus on real-world tasks, tools, safety practices, and technical accuracy.

Each category such as Electrician, Plumber, Welder, Mason, Carpenter, or AC Technician includes practical workshops that help students learn through real working conditions. Trainees get full exposure to tools, machines, repair techniques, installation procedures, and field-level problem solving.

After successful training, students receive a certificate and guidance for job placements. Many students trained under Skill Builder Institute have secured opportunities in India, Gulf countries, and various international projects. We focus on ensuring that every learner becomes confident, work-ready, and capable of delivering quality results.
