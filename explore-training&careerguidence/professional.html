HR, Management & Office Operations Training

This training program is designed for candidates who want to build a career in Human Resources, office administration, and management support roles. Learners are trained on real corporate workflows, office systems, documentation handling, reporting formats, and professional workplace behaviour.

HR training includes recruitment support, interview coordination, employee record management, attendance tracking, basic payroll understanding, and internal communication processes. Management modules focus on team coordination, task planning, follow-ups, email writing, meeting handling, and performance monitoring.

Complete hands-on training is provided by experienced professionals. After successful completion, students receive a recognised certificate and placement assistance for HR executive, admin assistant, office coordinator, and junior management roles at local, national, and international levels.

Sales, Accounts, Tally & Calling Training

This program prepares learners for sales, accounts, and business support roles required in modern organisations. Training focuses on customer communication, sales processes, lead handling, follow-ups, billing basics, and accuracy-based office work.

Tally and accounts modules cover accounting fundamentals, data entry, invoice preparation, GST basics, record maintenance, and daily accounting operations. Tally calling training includes professional calling techniques, customer interaction, objection handling, and data verification skills.

Practical training, live practice sessions, and real software exposure are provided. Certified candidates receive job assistance for sales executive, accounts assistant, Tally operator, calling executive, and office support roles in India and international markets.

BPO, Call Centre & Corporate Job Readiness Training

This training program is designed to make candidates job-ready for BPO, call centre, and corporate support roles. Learners are trained in communication skills, process understanding, system handling, and professional conduct required in corporate environments.

Training includes dialer systems, CRM tools, voice and non-voice processes, email and chat support, quality standards, and customer satisfaction practices. Mock calls, live simulations, and real-time practice sessions help build confidence and accuracy.

After completion, participants receive a professional certificate along with strong placement support. Job opportunities are provided from local companies to national and international organisations across BPO and corporate sectors.
