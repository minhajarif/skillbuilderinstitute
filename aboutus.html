About Skills Builder Institute

Who We Are

Skills Builder Institute is a vocational and professional training centre dedicated to delivering practical, job-oriented courses. We combine hands-on workshops with industry exposure to prepare learners for real work environments. Our programmes cover a wide range of technical, professional and soft-skill subjects designed for immediate employability and career advancement.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision:
To become a trusted and innovative skill development institute that empowers learners with practical skills for the modern workforce.
Mission:
  • Provide practical, hands-on training aligned with industry requirements.
  • Bridge the gap between education and employment with job-relevant skills.
  • Offer youth-targeted training that opens opportunities in local and international job markets.
  • Encourage continuous learning and professional growth through certifications and mentoring.

Our Achievements

  • Trained 1000+ learners across blue-collar and white-collar programmes.
  • Helped many candidates pass overseas skill tests and obtain GCC job placements.
  • High success rate for placement assistance among eligible participants.
  • Strong partnerships with industry recruiters and employers.

Courses Offered

Blue Collar / Technical Skill Programs

  • Electrician, Plumber, Pipe Fitter, Welder, Fabricator, Steel Fixer, Carpenter
  • AC Technician, MEP Foreman training, Mason, Painter, Helper and other trades
  • Saudi Skill Test preparation & practical job-readiness training

White Collar / Professional Programs

  • HR Trainee, Payroll Executive, Admin Executive, Store Keeper
  • Sales Executive, Digital Marketing, Business Communication, Tally
  • Call Centre / BPO training, Data Entry, Product & Business Analytics basics
