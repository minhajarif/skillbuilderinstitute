About Skills Builder Institute

Skills Builder Institute is a leading skill development centre in Siwan, dedicated to building career-ready professionals through hands-on training and personalised mentorship. Since our inception, we have trained over 10,000 learners and successfully placed more than 5,000 students in local and international roles. Our curriculum blends practical workshops, industry-oriented projects, and soft-skills coaching to ensure learners are work-ready.

Our approach

We focus on project-based learning, continuous feedback, and real employer expectations. Each course includes live sessions, recorded materials, assignments, and interview preparation. Trainers are selected for both technical expertise and teaching ability — they mentor each student until job-readiness. We support learners from registration through placement, ensuring a smooth career transition.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to cover Siwan and all of Bihar with high-quality skill training within five years. We aim to make vocational and professional education accessible, practical, and outcome-focused. Our mission is to empower learners with job-ready skills, ethical training, and long-term career growth.
